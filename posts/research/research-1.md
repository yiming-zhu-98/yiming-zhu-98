# [High-Frequency Normalizing Flow for Image Rescaling](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9826354)

## Abstract

This work propose an overall invertible framework with a conditional flow on the high-frequency space for High-quality image compression and restoration. 

## Methodology
We introduce a reference low-resolution (LR) manifold and propose a crossentropy Gaussian loss (CGloss) to force the downscaled manifold closer to the reference LR manifold and simultaneously fulfill recovering missing details. HfFlow can be generalized to other scale transformation tasks such as image colorization with its excellent rescaling capacity.

# 高频流函数解决图像缩放变换

## 摘要

本文提出了一种基于高频空间条件流的整体可逆框架，用于高质量图像压缩和恢复。

## 方法论
我们引入了一个参考低分辨率（LR）流形，并提出了一种交叉熵高斯损失函数（CGloss），以促使降尺度后的流形更接近参考LR流形，同时恢复缺失的细节。凭借其出色的重缩放能力，HfFlow可以推广到其他尺度变换任务，例如图像着色。
